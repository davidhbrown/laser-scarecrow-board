# laser-scarecrow-board
For Dr. Rebecca Brown's research testing the effectiveness of laser light in preventing bird damage in sweet corn

This repository contains files necessary to manufacture the main circuit board. At this point, it is not our intent to provide the original design files.

- **LaserScarecrow2020kit-mar.zip** -- Gerbers as provided to JLCPCB for board manufacturing
- **Component placement.pdf** -- diagram of where the various components go on the board
- **LS-Mainboard-2020.pdf** -- written instructions provided to student staff who assembled the finished boards
- **Board testing.pdf** -- diagram of where networks on board can be tested to check continuity
